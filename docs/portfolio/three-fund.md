# Three-fund

The Boglehead's [Three-fund portfolio](https://www.bogleheads.org/wiki/Three-fund_portfolio) model which tracks the following indexes:

- [Total Stock Market](../index/us-stock/#total-stock-market) Index or [S&P 500](../index/us-stock/#sp-500) Index
- [Total International Stock Market](../index/int-stock/#total-international-stock-market) Index
- [Total Bond Market](../index/us-bond/#total-bond-market) Index

The funds tracking these indexes should have low expense ratios, ideally no more than 0.10% (10 basis points) each.

An example portfolio of funds:

- [VTI](https://investor.vanguard.com/investment-products/etfs/profile/vti) - Vanguard Total Stock Market ETF
- [VXUS](https://investor.vanguard.com/investment-products/etfs/profile/vxus) - Vanguard Total International Stock ETF
- [BND](https://investor.vanguard.com/investment-products/etfs/profile/bnd) - Vanguard Total Bond Market ETF
