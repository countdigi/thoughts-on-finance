# World Stock

**Note**: *Work retirement plans usually offer individual US Stock and International Stock indexes only, however for individual accounts such as an IRA or Roth-IRA, these funds can help simplify your portfolio.*

## Overview

The World Stock Index holds virtually all of the publicly traded stocks in the world (~10,000 companies).

It combines the [Total Stock Market](../index/us-stock/#total-stock-market) Index and the [Total International Stock Market](../index/int-stock/#total-international-stock-market) Index into a single fund.

Currently the composition of a world fund is approximately:

- 60% US
- 40% International (ex-US)

This rebalances over time with the change in valuations of the US vs ex-US companies.

Funds typically track the **FTSE Global All Cap Index**.
