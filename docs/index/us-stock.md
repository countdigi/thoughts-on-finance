# US Stock

## Total Stock Market

### Overview

The Total Stock Market index holds all publicly traded companies headquartered and listed on stock exchanges in the United States (~3,500 companies).

Funds typically track either the **CRSP U.S. Total Market Index** or the **Dow Jones U.S. Total Stock Market Index**.


**Note**: If the Total Stock market Index is not available, you can substitute it with the [S&P 500](#sp-500) Index which is almost identical in long-term performance. Any fund tracking the **Russell 3000** Index is almost identical as well.

## S&P 500

### Overview

The S&P 500 Index is almost identical in long-term performance to [Total Stock Market](#total-stock-market) Index and can be used as a replacement when the other fund is unavailable.

It focuses specifically on the large-cap segment, capturing roughly 80% to 85% of the total value of the entire US equity market (~500 companies).
