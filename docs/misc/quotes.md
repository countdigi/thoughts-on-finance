# Quotes

*Owning the stock market over the long term is a winner’s game, but attempting to beat the market is a loser’s game.*

*The winning formula for success in investing is owning the entire stock market through an index fund, and then doing nothing.*

*We often say "don't just stand there - do something!" but in investing we should say "Don't do something - just stand there!"*

*Most people buy a stock and hope it goes up next week, and if it goes down they feel worse. I feel better. I think about what the company is going to be worth in 10 or 20 years, and I hope it goes down because I'll buy more!*

*You don't need to be a genius in what I do. You probably need an IQ of 120. And if its 170 you'll probably do worse.*
