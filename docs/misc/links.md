## Books

- [Tax Planning To and Through Early Retirement](https://www.amazon.com/Tax-Planning-Through-Early-Retirement/dp/B0FNNVXY16)
  by Sean Mullaney and Cody Garrett <br/>
  A great book that I view as "the missing link" for retirement and taxes. It also brings a refreshing take on how most retirees are actually taxed lighter in retirement. They are not anti-Roth but definitely counter the Roth-heavy advice often found in the financial advisor community.
